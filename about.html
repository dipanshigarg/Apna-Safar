Why We Exist

Building a safer, more transparent world for travelers.

The Reality of Travel Anxiety

We realized that for many—especially students and first-time travelers—the excitement of exploring a new city is often overshadowed by anxiety. Existing travel apps are great for booking tickets and hotels, but they vanish exactly when you need them most: when you are on the ground.

From navigating unsafe areas to avoiding tourist scams and overcharging, travelers are often left to figure it out alone. We believe you shouldn't have to choose between adventure and safety.

Our Mission

To provide a real-time safety and decision-support layer for travelers in unfamiliar cities.

Our Vision

To make unfamiliar travel feel more informed, transparent, and supported for every explorer.

Student-First

Built from real student travel experiences and challenges.

Trust & Transparency

We explain what we know, what we don’t, and why a signal matters.

Community Driven

Safety is a shared responsibility. We empower travelers to help each other.
