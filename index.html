Founder story

Why We Started Apna Safar

Apna Safar started with a familiar moment: arriving somewhere new and pretending to feel confident while quietly checking maps, prices, and faces around you. Travel apps helped us reach the city. They did not help us feel sure once we got there.

We are building the support layer travelers wish they had on the ground.

For every traveler who has stood outside a station and thought, “I just need to know what the safer choice is.”
